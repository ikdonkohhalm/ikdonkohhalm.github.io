Nice to meet you!

Isaac Donkoh-Halm has been an event coordinator for the Greater Lowell Area and Worcester Polytechnic Institute for over 5 years. From non-profit fundraisers to academic conferences, he has been recognized as a community leader since enrollment, and has been the recipient of 2 community leader awards. 

While much of his work are events focused on video games, he has also done work as an archivist and video content creator for the Weiss Jazz History Database, the Co-Chair of WPI's SocComm Films, and the president of Diversity in Games.

Outside of school, he works as an assistant to the director for the African Community Center of Lowell.
